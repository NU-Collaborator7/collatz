Collatz Conjecture Simulator

The Collatz Conjecture states that for any positive integer, if you repeat the operation of dividing it by 2 if it is even, or multiplying it by 3 and adding 1 if it is odd, it will always eventually reach 1. Proposed by Lothar Collatz in 1937.
